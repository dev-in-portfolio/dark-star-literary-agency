# Launch Visibility Rules

This document describes how future content should be handled on the public site.

## Publicly Visible

- Live books
- Live collections
- Available learning lines
- Real downloads
- Official brand pages

## Keep Hidden Until Ready

- Future series pages
- Future collection pages
- Future learning line pages
- Future book pages
- Free download pages that are not yet ready
- Rights / licensing pages unless they are intentionally launched
- Press kit pages unless they are intentionally launched
- School / library pages unless they are intentionally launched
- Brand guidelines unless they are intentionally launched

## Release Rule

Future content may be built internally ahead of time, but it should not be added to public navigation until it is ready for purchase, preorder, sample download, or an intentional teaser.
