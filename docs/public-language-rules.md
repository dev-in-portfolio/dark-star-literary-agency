# Public Language Rules

Use public-facing terms like:

- Series
- Collections
- Book lines
- Learning lines
- Activity collections
- Lulu & Ellie Library
- Lulu & Ellie Universe

Do not use "branches" in public-facing copy.

Branch codes can still exist internally for production, planning, and organization, but they should not appear on the public website.

## Public Safety Rule

Public navigation should only show pages or items that are:

- Live for purchase
- Available for preorder
- Supported by a free sample or intentional preview
- Intentionally announced as coming soon
