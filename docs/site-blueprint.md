# Site Blueprint

Dark Star Literary Agency is a public-facing launch site for three connected brands:

- Dark Star Literary Agency - the professional umbrella
- Ambrose Caspian Vale - the author brand
- Lulu & Ellie Adventures - the flagship children's universe

## Current Page Strategy

The site is a single-page landing experience with anchor navigation.

Sections:

1. Hero
2. Three brand portal cards
3. About Dark Star
4. Meet Ambrose Caspian Vale
5. Lulu & Ellie Adventures
6. Public catalog preview
7. Parents & Teachers promise
8. Free downloads / coming soon
9. Contact
10. Footer

## Design Direction

- Midnight navy and black-blue background
- Soft gold accents
- Ivory text
- Subtle star and portal glow
- Premium literary styling with warmer child-friendly accents inside Lulu & Ellie content

## Public Positioning

The site should feel polished, literary, and magical immediately, while keeping future collections hidden until they are live, preorder-ready, sample-ready, or intentionally teased.
