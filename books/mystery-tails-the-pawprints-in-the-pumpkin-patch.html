The Pawprints in the Pumpkin Patch

Odd pawprints appear between the pumpkins, and the mystery turns out to be a lesson in noticing what a patch has been trying to say.

The Pawprints in the Pumpkin Patch is part of Lulu & Ellie Mystery Tails. Here, odd pawprints appear between the pumpkins, and the mystery turns out to be a lesson in noticing what a patch has been trying to say. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

Something has walked through the pumpkin patch, but the prints don’t quite belong to any creature the sisters know. Lulu leaps to a dramatic answer while Ellie notices the shape, spacing, and timing of the prints, and the truth turns out to be gentler than the clues first suggest.

Young readers can settle into a series that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
