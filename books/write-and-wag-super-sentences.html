Super Sentences

A sentence practice book that helps children move from short words into proud, readable sentences.

Super Sentences is part of Lulu & Ellie Write & Wag. Here, a sentence practice book that helps children move from short words into proud, readable sentences. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

Super Sentences invites children to stretch from words into full thoughts, one careful line at a time. The book helps writers notice capitals, spaces, punctuation, and the satisfying feeling of finishing a complete sentence.

Young readers can settle into a workbook line that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
