The Clock-Tide Crossing

A crossing that answers to both clock and tide sends Lulu and Ellie through a rescue mission that bends time just enough to matter.

The Clock-Tide Crossing is part of Lulu & Ellie in Space. Here, a crossing that answers to both clock and tide sends Lulu and Ellie through a rescue mission that bends time just enough to matter. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A crossing that listens to clocks and tides at once asks the sisters to keep pace with a rhythm that changes by the minute. The rescue teaches the sisters that time can be measured in more than one way, and that care sometimes means waiting for the right moment.

Young readers can settle into a series that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
