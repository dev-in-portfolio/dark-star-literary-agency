The Campfire That Opened a Door

A campfire door opens onto a new trail, and the sisters must learn how to step into the woods without losing their way.

The Campfire That Opened a Door is part of Lulu & Ellie Go To Camp. Here, a campfire door opens onto a new trail, and the sisters must learn how to step into the woods without losing their way. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A campfire crackles, the sparks become a doorway, and Lulu and Ellie are invited into a trail that hasn’t finished telling its story. The adventure asks the sisters to work as a team, listen to the woods, and discover that camp courage often begins with staying together.

Young readers can settle into a series that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
