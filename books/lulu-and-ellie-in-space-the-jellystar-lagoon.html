The Jellystar Lagoon

A lagoon made of jellystar light turns a rescue into a shimmering, laughter-filled space voyage.

The Jellystar Lagoon is part of Lulu & Ellie in Space. Here, a lagoon made of jellystar light turns a rescue into a shimmering, laughter-filled space voyage. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A lagoon of jellystar light ripples beside the U.F.O., and Lulu and Ellie are pulled into a rescue that sparkles and sways. Lumo and Sally help the sisters learn that some worlds need care more than speed, and that a gentle rescue can still feel enormous.

Young readers can settle into a series that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.

What Young Readers Will Love

What Young Readers Will Love

  • A story shaped around space wonder and the kind of problem children can follow without getting overwhelmed
  • Lulu’s bold energy and Ellie’s careful noticing
  • A world with clues, maps, helpers, or quiet details that reward attention

What Parents and Teachers Can Expect
