Lulu & Ellie and the Moonlit Circus

A traveling circus glows under the moon, and Lulu and Ellie must help the show find its lost sense of wonder.

Lulu & Ellie and the Moonlit Circus is part of Lulu & Ellie Adventures. Here, a traveling circus glows under the moon, and Lulu and Ellie must help the show find its lost sense of wonder. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A moonlit circus has lost the bright rhythm that makes it sparkle, and the sisters arrive just as the performers begin to worry. The mystery is less about what is wrong than about what the circus needs to remember about itself, and Lulu and Ellie help the whole show breathe again.

Young readers can settle into a storybook adventure that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.

What Young Readers Will Love
