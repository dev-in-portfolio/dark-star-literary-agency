The Case of the Missing Mooncake

A mooncake disappears from a lantern-lit table, and Ellie’s careful clues reveal that the real answer is sweeter than suspicion.

The Case of the Missing Mooncake is part of Lulu & Ellie Mystery Tails. Here, a mooncake disappears from a lantern-lit table, and Ellie’s careful clues reveal that the real answer is sweeter than suspicion. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A mooncake vanishes at exactly the moment the lanterns come on, and the whole case feels far bigger than one missing treat. Lulu makes a few funny guesses, Ellie follows the crumbs, and the sisters discover that a misunderstanding can look much stranger than it really is.

Young readers can settle into a series that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
