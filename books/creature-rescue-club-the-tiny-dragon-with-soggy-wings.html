The Tiny Dragon with Soggy Wings

A small dragon cannot quite lift off until Lulu and Ellie discover that soggy wings need drying, patience, and the right kind of nest.

The Tiny Dragon with Soggy Wings is part of Lulu & Ellie Creature Rescue Club. Here, a small dragon cannot quite lift off until Lulu and Ellie discover that soggy wings need drying, patience, and the right kind of nest. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A tiny dragon shivers in the rain, and the sisters learn that rescue begins with warmth and the right kind of shelter. The dragon is not broken; it is simply wet, worried, and waiting for someone gentle enough to understand what help looks like.

Young readers can settle into a series that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
