The Talent Show Under the Stars

A camp talent show under the stars becomes a celebration of teamwork, creativity, and the bravery of trying in public.

The Talent Show Under the Stars is part of Lulu & Ellie Go To Camp. Here, a camp talent show under the stars becomes a celebration of teamwork, creativity, and the bravery of trying in public. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A talent show under the stars asks the whole camp to be brave in front of one another. The sisters learn that camp courage can mean supporting a friend, taking a turn, or simply showing up with a willing heart.

Young readers can settle into a series that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
