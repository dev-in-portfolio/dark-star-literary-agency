Lulu & Ellie and the Island That Drifted Away

Floating islands, windships, and a storm wall that can only be crossed by following the invisible currents beneath it.

Lulu & Ellie and the Island That Drifted Away is part of Lulu & Ellie Adventures. Here, floating islands, windships, and a storm wall that can only be crossed by following the invisible currents beneath it. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

An island drifts away from its place in the sky, and the sisters must learn the shape of a path they cannot see. Kiri the cloudbird and Thundra the thunderbird help Lulu and Ellie discover that crooked paths may be following something invisible and true.

Young readers can settle into a storybook adventure that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
