Loops, Letters, and Pawprints

A cursive practice book that turns every loop into a small clue and every line into a pawprint of progress.

Loops, Letters, and Pawprints is part of Lulu & Ellie Cursive Club. Here, a cursive practice book that turns every loop into a small clue and every line into a pawprint of progress. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

Loops, Letters, and Pawprints makes cursive feel like a cozy trail of connected marks. Children can practice the connections, notice the shapes, and feel the satisfaction of following a line that keeps going without breaking.

Young readers can settle into a learning line that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
