Lulu & Ellie and the Snow Dragon’s Bell

A snowy mountain kingdom, a shy snow dragon, and a bell that can wake the right kind of magic.

Lulu & Ellie and the Snow Dragon’s Bell is part of Lulu & Ellie Adventures. Here, a snowy mountain kingdom, a shy snow dragon, and a bell that can wake the right kind of magic. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A mountain bell has gone quiet, and the silence reaches all the way to the snowy kingdom where a shy dragon waits for someone to listen. Niva the shy snow dragon and Boreal the misunderstood frost bear need help, but the real key is a quiet voice that can wake the magic without startling it.

Young readers can settle into a storybook adventure that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.
