Lulu & Ellie and the Star Map of Everywhere

A sky map that seems to reach farther than any single night, guiding the sisters toward a secret made of constellations.

Lulu & Ellie and the Star Map of Everywhere is part of Lulu & Ellie Adventures. Here, a sky map that seems to reach farther than any single night, guiding the sisters toward a secret made of constellations. The adventure stays warm and readable, even when the problem feels delightfully strange.

A star map appears to point beyond every familiar sky, and Lulu and Ellie realize that the map is asking for more than one kind of journey. The sisters follow the stars without trying to own them, and the answer becomes a lesson about wonder, navigation, and where a story can go next.

Young readers can settle into a storybook adventure that gives them a clear emotional shape: a problem to understand, a world worth helping, and a finish that feels kind.

What Young Readers Will Love

What Young Readers Will Love

  • A story shaped around wonder and the kind of problem children can follow without getting overwhelmed
