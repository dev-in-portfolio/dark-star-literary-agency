About Dark Star Literary Agency

Dark Star Literary Agency develops imaginative children’s book projects with strong character continuity, clear production systems, and long-term creative growth.

Its flagship universe is Lulu & Ellie Adventures, created by Ambrose Caspian Vale.

A good story can become a whole world, and a good world can help children read, imagine, learn, laugh, and grow.

Dark Star Literary Agency is currently focused on in-house publishing, creative development, and owned literary properties. Outside representation and submissions may be added in the future.

Lulu & Ellie Adventures

Two scruffy sisters. One magical portal. A universe of cozy quests.

Lulu and Ellie are two scruffy terrier sisters with one very unusual problem: magical portals keep finding them.

Lulu

Small, tan-golden, scruffy, bold, dramatic, snack-motivated, and usually ready to leap before reading the map. Lulu wears a red bandana and tends to charge into the story with excellent confidence and questionable planning.

Ellie

Slightly larger, cream-colored, careful, observant, and very good at noticing the clue everyone else missed. Ellie wears a blue bandana and keeps the whole adventure quietly on track.

Together, they explore, solve, and learn.

They travel into magical worlds, solve gentle mysteries, help creatures in trouble, earn charms and badges, and learn that bravery can look different every time.

Sometimes courage is loud. Sometimes courage is quiet. Sometimes courage is checking the map before Lulu runs into a mushroom maze.

Parents & Teachers

Gentle books and activities for curious young readers.

Lulu & Ellie books are designed to be warm, playful, encouraging, and emotionally safe.

The stories and activities use curiosity, humor, kindness, and problem-solving instead of fear, shame, or pressure.

The goal is simple: help children feel brave, capable, curious, and ready to try.

  • Reading confidence
  • Gentle emotional lessons
  • Low-pressure learning
  • Creative thinking
  • Kindness and problem-solving
  • Warm adventure without harsh fear
  • Story-powered workbooks and activities
