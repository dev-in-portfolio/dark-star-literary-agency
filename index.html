Why the world keeps growing

Why the world keeps growing.

The Dark Star universe is designed to expand in a way that still feels emotionally coherent. Each new story can introduce a new world, helper, or challenge without losing the warmth that anchors the series.

That flexibility gives the world room for storybooks, learning lines, companion guides, future play formats, and seasonal books while keeping Lulu and Ellie at the center of the experience.
