About Dark Star Literary Agency

Dark Star Literary Agency develops imaginative children’s book projects with strong character continuity, clear production systems, and long-term creative growth.

Its flagship universe is Lulu & Ellie Adventures, created by Ambrose Caspian Vale.

A good story can become a whole world, and a good world can help children read, imagine, learn, laugh, and grow.

Dark Star Literary Agency is currently focused on in-house publishing, creative development, and owned literary properties. Outside representation and submissions may be added in the future.

Ambrose

Meet Ambrose Caspian Vale.

Author of the Lulu & Ellie Adventures and magical books for curious young readers.

Magical stories with a gentle compass.

Ambrose Caspian Vale writes children’s stories filled with brave little dogs, impossible portals, cozy mysteries, gentle lessons, and worlds where courage does not always have to roar.

As the creator of the Lulu & Ellie Adventures, Ambrose builds books where children can explore friendship, problem-solving, kindness, creativity, careful thinking, and wonder through warm, imaginative adventures.

Parents & Teachers

Gentle books and bonus pages for curious young readers.

Lulu & Ellie books are designed to be warm, playful, encouraging, and emotionally safe.

The stories and companion pages use curiosity, humor, kindness, and problem-solving instead of fear, shame, or pressure.

The goal is simple: help children feel brave, capable, curious, and ready to try.

  • Reading confidence
  • Gentle emotional lessons
  • Low-pressure learning
  • Creative thinking
  • Kindness and problem-solving
  • Warm adventure without harsh fear
  • Story-powered workbooks and book fun pages
