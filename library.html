How to use the library

Start with the flagship series if you want the core world, then move into the learning lines if you want books that support specific kinds of reading, writing, or practice.

The library is organized so readers can explore by format, purpose, or mood without losing the connection back to the main universe.

How to Begin

How to begin

If you want the heart of the universe, begin with Lulu & Ellie Adventures. If you want a specific reading or learning experience, choose a storybook series or learning line from the sections above, then open the book pages that belong to that line.

If you are visiting for school, library, or family browsing, the library gives you the quickest overview of what each line does and how it connects to the broader world before you dive into the deeper catalog pages.
