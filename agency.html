What Dark Star Is

What Dark Star is

Dark Star Literary Agency is an independent literary studio, publishing imprint, and creative development home for immersive children’s book worlds. It exists to guide ideas from a strong concept into a connected, expandable universe.

The agency identity gives the overall project a clear public home while the author brand and the Lulu & Ellie universe each carry their own voices, visual language, and purpose.

The Heart of the Imprint

Editorial focus

Dark Star projects are shaped around character continuity, a clear emotional tone, and a structure that supports future expansion without losing reader trust.

That means the books can grow from a single story into a larger family of series, learning tools, and companion books while still feeling like they belong to the same world.

Publishing Philosophy

Publishing philosophy

A good publishing house does more than release books. It gives the work a shape, a promise, and a visible home that readers can return to with confidence.

Dark Star is built to support that kind of continuity: gentle creative growth, clear story identity, and a public-facing world that can be understood at a glance.

A Home for Lulu & Ellie

Flagship property

Lulu & Ellie Adventures is the flagship Dark Star universe: a growing children’s world of magical portals, cozy mysteries, creature rescues, learning lines, companion books, and gentle courage.

It is the clearest example of how the agency, author brand, and storyworld fit together as one public-facing creative system.
