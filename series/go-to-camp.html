Step Inside the Story

Step Inside the Story

Each page works as a second-level catalog page, giving readers a clear doorway into the books that belong here.

Go To Camp brings the portal universe into trails, cabins, canoes, campfires, talent shows, maps, teamwork, and patch-earning adventures. The stories are warm, funny, and safe.

The camp challenges focus on confidence, cooperation, and curiosity rather than pressure.

What Readers Will Find

Readers will find camp patches, trails, cabins, and canoes.

Readers will find teamwork and trying again.

Why Families Will Love It
