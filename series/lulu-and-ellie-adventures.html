Step Inside the Story

Step Inside the Story

Each page works as a second-level catalog page, giving readers a clear doorway into the books that belong here.

The flagship series follows two scruffy terrier sisters as magical portals carry them into strange worlds where something has gone gently wrong.

Each story begins with wonder, moves through clues and choices, and ends with Lulu and Ellie helping a world become safer, kinder, or better understood.

What Readers Will Find

What Readers Will Find

Readers will find portal worlds, charms, and maps.

Readers will find lulu’s bold leaps and Ellie’s careful noticing.

Readers will find gentle mysteries that reward attention.

Readers will find return visits that still feel fresh.

Why Families Will Love It

Why Families Will Love It

Families will love warm adventures that still feel big enough to matter.

Families will love a steady emotional center for read-aloud time.

Families will love recurring clues and story objects that children can track.

Families will love a world that grows without losing its welcome.

Signature Elements

What Makes This Line Special

One signature element is portals that open in unexpected places.

One signature element is recurring charms, maps, and helper characters.

One signature element is worlds that need understanding more than conquering.

One signature element is warm endings that make room for another visit.
