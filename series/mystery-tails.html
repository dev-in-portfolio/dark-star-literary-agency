Step Inside the Story

Step Inside the Story

Each page works as a second-level catalog page, giving readers a clear doorway into the books that belong here.

Mystery Tails turns mystery into something warm, clever, and safe. Lulu and Ellie follow clues, notice what changed, listen to unusual helpers, and discover that many mysteries are not about villains.

These books are built around confusion, missing context, and the idea that careful thinking can be kinder than quick judgment.

What Readers Will Find

What Readers Will Find

Readers will find clues, maps, and case tags.

Readers will find misunderstandings instead of scary crime.

Readers will find ellie’s detail-hunting and Lulu’s funny wrong guesses.

Readers will find warm endings rooted in listening.

Why Families Will Love It
