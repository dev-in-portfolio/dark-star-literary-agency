Step Inside the Story

Step Inside the Story

Each page works as a second-level catalog page, giving readers a clear doorway into the books that belong here.

Backyard Academy turns ordinary outdoor discoveries into magical learning moments. With Professor Acorn as a tiny nature guide, Lulu and Ellie learn to observe, compare, ask questions, and notice how much wonder lives close to home.

The books are built to make learning feel like noticing, not testing.

What Readers Will Find

What Readers Will Find

Readers will find rocks, seeds, clouds, bugs, weather, roots, and soil.
