Lulu & Ellie Adventures

Two scruffy sisters. One magical portal. A universe of cozy quests.

Lulu and Ellie are two scruffy terrier sisters with one very unusual problem: magical portals keep finding them.

Some portals glow inside old maps. Some shimmer in gardens. Some open near bells, books, trails, stars, puddles, pumpkins, moonlight, or things that definitely were not glowing a minute ago.

Wherever the doorway appears, one thing is always true: somewhere on the other side, a world needs help.

Together
They help worlds out of trouble.
Flagship universe

They travel into magical worlds, solve gentle mysteries, help creatures in trouble, earn charms and badges, and learn that bravery can look different every time.

How the Adventures Begin

How the adventures begin

Every Lulu & Ellie adventure begins when something magical finds them. A portal opens. A charm glows. A clue appears. A creature needs help. A world is out of balance. Lulu and Ellie must explore, listen, learn, and help set things right.

What young readers will find

Portal worlds, recurring charms, cozy problem-solving, and warm endings that reward care, listening, and courage.

Recurring Story Elements

Recurring story elements

Portals

Magical openings that carry Lulu and Ellie into new worlds.

Charms

Small story objects that guide the adventure and help mark progress.

Clues

Visual and narrative details that reward careful looking.

Badges

Tokens of progress, teamwork, or bravery earned along the way.

Maps

Helpful routes, world diagrams, and orientation tools that support the story.

Helpers

Friendly characters, creatures, and guides who deepen the universe.

Gentle mysteries

Problems that ask for listening, context, and kindness rather than fear.

Meet Lulu

Meet Lulu

Small, tan-golden, scruffy, bold, dramatic, snack-motivated, and usually ready to leap before reading the map.

Lulu wears a red bandana and tends to charge into the story with excellent confidence and questionable planning.

Meet Ellie

Meet Ellie

Slightly larger, cream-colored, careful, observant, and very good at noticing the clue everyone else missed.

Ellie wears a blue bandana and keeps the whole adventure quietly on track.

How the Universe Works

How the universe works

Sometimes courage is loud. Sometimes courage is quiet. Sometimes courage is checking the map before Lulu runs into a mushroom maze.

The universe blends magical portals, cozy mysteries, creature rescues, learning workbooks, companion books, and future play formats into one connected storyworld.

What Young Readers Will Find

What young readers will find

Children step into adventures that feel playful and safe. They get clues to follow, helpers to meet, and gentle problems that are solved through attention, empathy, and teamwork.

The result is a world that can be exciting without becoming overwhelming. Children can laugh at Lulu, admire Ellie, and keep returning to the same story universe with confidence.

What Families Will Love

What families will love

Parents can expect gentle pacing, emotionally safe storytelling, and a focus on curiosity rather than fear. The books are meant to support warm read-aloud time and comfortable independent exploration.

The universe is structured so new series, learning lines, and activities still feel connected to the same familiar world.
